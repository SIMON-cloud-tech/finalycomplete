Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This page explains how we handle your data securely and transparently.

1. Information We Collect

We collect basic information such as your name, email, and enquiry details when you use our contact form or register on the platform. All data is stored securely in audit-ready files and databases.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process bookings, payments, and landlord payouts. We also use it to send confirmation emails and provide support. We never sell or share your personal data with unauthorized third parties.

3. Security Measures

We sanitize and validate all inputs to prevent XSS or injection attacks. Passwords are hashed before storage, and sensitive credentials are loaded securely from environment variables. Transactions are logged for transparency and auditability.

4. Payments & Landlord Payouts

Payment data is processed securely via M-Pesa integration. Landlord payouts are remitted through Paybill, Till, or direct phone transfers. All transactions are recorded in payments.json, sales.json, and landlords.json for accountability.

5. Transparency & Auditability

Every action — from registration to payment — is recorded. This ensures that auditors, collaborators, and users can trace activities clearly. Our design enforces accountability and builds trust across all stakeholders.
