Commission Policy & Terms

This page explains why the business sets commission deductions, the terms and conditions for usage, and the freedom landlords have to choose whether to use the system.

1. Why Commission Is Required

The commission charged by the business ensures sustainability of the platform, covering transaction processing, system maintenance, customer support, and continuous improvements. Without this deduction, the system cannot guarantee secure payouts, audit-ready records, and reliable service for all users.

2. Terms & Conditions for Businesses

Businesses using this platform agree that commissions are deducted automatically from tenant payments before landlord payouts. The rate is set by the business and may vary depending on operational costs and service agreements. All deductions are transparent and recorded in payments.json and sales.json for accountability.

3. Landlord Freedom of Choice

Landlords are free to decide whether to use this system. No one is compelled to adopt it, and participation is entirely voluntary. By choosing to use the platform, landlords accept the commission deductions as part of the service agreement. Those who prefer alternative arrangements may opt out without penalty.

4. Transparency & Accountability

Every transaction — from tenant payment to landlord payout — is logged and auditable. This ensures that landlords, businesses, and auditors can clearly trace deductions and payouts. The design enforces accountability and builds trust across all stakeholders.

5. Client Complaints & Landlord Conduct

The business reserves the right to investigate repeated client complaints about a specific landlord. If such complaints are verified and deemed serious, the landlord may be permanently restricted from using the system. This measure protects tenants, preserves trust, and ensures the platform remains fair and reliable for all stakeholders.

6. Fair Use & Accountability

Landlords are expected to uphold professional standards when using the system. Any misuse, fraudulent activity, or consistent negative feedback from clients will be treated as a breach of terms. The business may suspend or terminate access without refund to safeguard the integrity of the platform. By choosing to use the system, landlords agree to these conditions and accept responsibility for their conduct.

7. Client Responsibilities

Clients are required to sign out of the system when leaving a house to ensure accurate records and proper release of the booking. This prevents unauthorized use and maintains transparency between tenants, landlords, and the business.

8. Payment Conditions

Clients must not make any payments unless they have communicated directly with the landlord and received onboarding or confirmation for the specific house they are booking. This ensures that tenants only pay for verified bookings and prevents fraudulent or mistaken transactions.

9. Why Deposits Are Required

Deposits are collected at the time of booking to secure the house and protect both tenants and landlords. For tenants, the deposit guarantees that the house will be reserved and not offered to others while onboarding is completed. For landlords, it provides assurance that the tenant is committed to the booking. Deposits are recorded transparently and may be refundable according to the landlord’s terms.
