How the Real Estate Management System Works

This platform connects businesses, landlords, and clients into one seamless ecosystem. Every action is secure, auditable, and designed for transparency.

1. Security & Data Protection

The system is built with a privacy-first approach. All inputs are validated and sanitized to prevent XSS or injection attacks. Passwords are hashed before storage, and sensitive credentials (like M-Pesa and SendGrid keys) are loaded securely from environment variables. Every transaction is logged in JSON files, ensuring auditability and traceability.

2. Business Dashboard

Administrators oversee the entire platform through the business dashboard. They can monitor listings, landlords, payments, bookings, valuation, revenue, and analytics. This ensures profitability, compliance, and operational transparency.

3. Landlord Onboarding & Payouts

Landlords register securely and access their personalized dashboard. They can manage property listings, track payments, and view analytics. When clients make payments, the system calculates commissions and remits landlord payouts via M-Pesa. Payouts can be directed to Paybill, Till, or directly to a phone number using B2B or B2C integrations. Balances are updated instantly in landlords.json, ensuring landlords always see accurate earnings.

4. Client Journey

Clients browse available houses in the shop using a responsive, auto-sliding grid. They can book properties, confirm details, and proceed to checkout. Bookings are validated and stored in bookings.json, ensuring accuracy and trust. Clients receive instant feedback on successful payments, along with confirmation emails.

5. Payment Flow

During checkout, the system validates the booking, calculates commission, and remits landlord payouts via M-Pesa. Transactions are recorded in payments.json, sales.json, and landlords.json. This ensures financial accountability. Clients see immediate confirmation, while landlords’ balances update in real time.

6. Auditability & Transparency

Every step — from registration to payment — is recorded. JSON-based storage ensures that auditors and collaborators can trace actions clearly. This design enforces accountability and builds trust across all stakeholders. The system is designed to be scalable, secure, and transparent for long-term growth.

This data is thereafter migrated to the cloud via a non-relational database where it is stored to be used by the system at any time of need
